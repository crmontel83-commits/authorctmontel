About

Behind the pages

My name is C. T. Montel, and I suffer from a beautiful form of madness—that of the creative. From the moment I was born, my mind churned with the glimmers of fantastical worlds awaiting only to be explored.


I write high, epic, and dark fantasy that asks what happens when absolute evil is not a story told around the fire, but a living force intent on grinding heroes into dust. My work lives in The Chronicles of Ka'Maetheus, a sprawling universe of broken gods, haunted cities, and the stubborn souls who refuse to bow.


If you enjoy character-driven stories, impossible odds, and that tiny, treacherous spark of hope that survives in the shadow of oblivion, you're in the right place.

Story Vibes

  • Epic quests in crumbling worlds
  • Ordinary people facing impossible evil
  • Moral ambiguity, costly magic, hard choices
  • A stubborn ember of hope in all the ruin

The World of Pasi

The World of Pasi · Gateway to Ka'Maetheus

The Chronicles of Ka'Maetheus span many worlds—shattered realms, forgotten spheres, and places that should have stayed myth. Pasi is where you enter: a bruised planet caught between fading gods, hungry empires, and the quiet, stubborn lives that refuse to be erased.


Think of this space as your first gate. From here, you'll travel through Pasi's cities and wastelands, learn the rules of its magic, and hear the stories children whisper when the lamps go out. Other worlds in Ka'Maetheus wait beyond the fog—but we begin here.

Worldbuilding

Pasi is a world built on fault lines—between old faiths and new powers, between the civilized glow of walled cities and the wild dark that presses in from the borders.

  • Borderland towns that cling to trade routes like lifelines.
  • Ruined sites where something ancient clearly lost.
  • Orders and societies trying to contain what slips through the cracks.

The further you travel from the safe roads, the more the seams of reality show. Monsters aren't always obvious. Neither are heroes.

Magic System

On Pasi, power is never free. Magic moves through pacts, marks, and the old echoes of Ka'Maetheus itself. Every working has a cost—in memory, in blood, in the slow erosion of who you think you are.

  • Some orders bind themselves to ancient beings for borrowed strength.
  • Some carve sigils into steel, bone, or their own skin.
  • Some pretend there is no magic at all—until it comes collecting.

The Hunters who walk this world don't just fight monsters. They wrestle with the price of the power they use to face them.

Fairy Tales & Mythologies

The stories told to Pasi's children are not soft. Their lullabies remember cities that vanished overnight, rivers that ran backwards for a year, and saints who broke the sky to keep something worse from getting in.

  • Whispered tales of doors that appear only on storm nights.
  • Fables about bargains you must never strike—and what happens if you do.
  • Local myths that hint at the larger machinery of Ka'Maetheus.

As you read, you'll see these tales surface in tavern gossip, Hunter training, and the quiet fears of ordinary people. Pasi's myths are the breadcrumb trail to the deeper horrors, and wonders, of the wider universe.
