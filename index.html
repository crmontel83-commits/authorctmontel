About

Behind the pages

My name is C. T. Montel, and I suffer from a beautiful form of madness—that of the creative. From the moment I was born, my mind churned with the glimmers of fantastical worlds awaiting only to be explored.


I write high, epic, and dark fantasy that asks what happens when absolute evil is not a story told around the fire, but a living force intent on grinding heroes into dust. My work lives in The Chronicles of Ka'Maetheus, a sprawling universe of broken gods, haunted cities, and the stubborn souls who refuse to bow.


If you enjoy character-driven stories, impossible odds, and that tiny, treacherous spark of hope that survives in the shadow of oblivion, you're in the right place.

Story Vibes

  • Epic quests in crumbling worlds
  • Ordinary people facing impossible evil
  • Moral ambiguity, costly magic, hard choices
  • A stubborn ember of hope in all the ruin
